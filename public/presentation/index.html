Set Up Your Profile

  • Keywords - Add service-specific keywords (e.g., "janitorial," "HVAC," "facility maintenance") for semantic matching beyond just NAICS

The AI match score is calculated from multiple weighted factors:

  • Past performance (15%) - Similar work you've successfully completed before
  • Competition level (10%) - Set-aside type and estimated number of competitors

AI Learning

As you import opportunities and record win/loss outcomes, the scoring algorithm learns your actual success patterns and adjusts weights to better predict which opportunities you're most likely to win.

AI-Powered Q&A Management

The AI reads your documents, generates clarification questions, and auto-answers subcontractor inquiries. Questions it can't answer are escalated.

Clarification Questions - AI Q&A System

19 AI-generated questions organized by trade, category, and priority

How the AI Q&A System Works

Phase 1: AI Generates Questions

After analyzing your SOW, the AI identifies gaps, ambiguities, and missing information from a subcontractor's perspective. For each question, it provides:

  • Priority Level - Critical, High, Medium, or Low based on cost impact
  • Category - Missing specifications, unclear frequencies, missing quantities
  • Trade Assignment - Which trade the question applies to (HVAC, Electrical, Fire Safety, etc.)
  • Source Reference - Exact page and section in the SOW where the ambiguity exists
  • Impact Statement - "If not clarified: the subcontractor cannot accurately price the filter replacements"

AI Auto-Answer vs. Escalation

How Procuvex decides whether to answer a question automatically or escalate it to you.

AI Can Auto-Answer When:

  • The answer exists in the documentation - If a subcontractor asks "What are the HVAC inspection requirements?" and the SOW clearly states "Monthly inspection of all rooftop units," the AI provides the answer with a source citation
  • Historical Q&A has the answer - If a similar question was asked and answered on a previous project, the AI references that answer
  • Industry standards apply - For questions about compliance standards (NFPA, OSHA, EPA), the AI can reference the relevant code
  • Contract terms are explicit - Questions about payment terms, insurance requirements, or scheduling that are stated in the contract documents

Auto-Answer Result

The subcontractor gets an immediate response with a citation back to the source document. No waiting for the PM to review and respond.

AI Escalates When:

  • The information is genuinely missing - The SOW doesn't specify filter sizes, equipment quantities, or specific scheduling requirements
  • The question requires judgment - "Can we substitute Brand X for Brand Y?" requires a human decision
  • Pricing/budget questions - "What is the budget range for this scope?" is never auto-answered
  • Scope interpretation - When the SOW is ambiguous about whether a service is included or excluded
  • Timeline negotiations - "Can the mobilization period be extended?" requires project manager input

Escalation Result

The question appears in the project manager's dashboard as "Pending - Needs Human Review." The PM answers it once, and that answer becomes part of the AI's knowledge base for future similar questions.
